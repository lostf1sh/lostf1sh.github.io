# i'm 18 now

turned 18 today.

i kept expecting to feel different. some switch flipping at midnight. it didn't. same room, same projects open, same half-finished repos staring back at me. i woke up and the little age counter on the homepage just rolled over.

writing it down anyway, because the small stuff adds up even when no single day feels like much.

started taking this seriously a few years back. picked up linux properly somewhere in here. broke things, fixed them, broke them again. most of what i know now i didn't a couple years ago — i only really notice it looking back.

so the honest version: i don't have it figured out, and i'm not supposed to yet. the plan is the same as yesterday. keep building small things. ship more than i polish. stay curious.

eighteen just means i get to keep doing that with fewer excuses.

-- moli
